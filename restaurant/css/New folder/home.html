Pork shawarma is a variation of the traditional Middle Eastern dish, typically featuring thinly sliced, marinated pork that is roasted on a vertical rotisserie. The succulent pork is often seasoned with a blend of spices such as cumin, paprika, garlic, and coriander, and served wrapped in flatbread with various toppings and sauces for a delicious and flavorful meal.

Beef shawarma is a savory Middle Eastern dish featuring thinly sliced, marinated beef that is traditionally cooked on a vertical rotisserie. The tender, seasoned beef is often served wrapped in flatbread with a variety of accompaniments such as tahini sauce, vegetables, and pickles, offering a flavorful and satisfying dining experience.

Chicken shawarma is a Middle Eastern dish of seasoned, marinated chicken cooked on a vertical rotisserie, resulting in tender meat with a crispy exterior. Served in pita bread or on a platter with accompaniments like tahini sauce, it's known for its bold spices and juicy texture, enjoyed globally as a popular street food and restaurant staple.
